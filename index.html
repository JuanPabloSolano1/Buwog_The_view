BUWOG THE VIEW-
GRANTS SOUTHWATERN BERLIN AN ARCHITECTURAL HIGHLIGHT ON
THE BANKS OF THE DAHME.

The ensemble is located in residential quarter 52º Nord. It brings together a total of 63 condominiums housed in three buildings, with every apartment boasting a direct view over the water. Directly in front of the ensemble runs a peaceful, 600-metre-long promenade for rest and relaxation. BUWOG THE VIEW consists of three separate buildings (House I, House II, House III) and represents the latest construction phase in the ongoing development of 52º Nord. Project Highlights:

  • Water views for each and every apartment
  • Master bedrooms with ensuite bathrooms
  • Underground parking places optionally available (for electric cars on request)

House I and III

Houses I and III are designed by renowned architectural firm PA GmbH Pätzold Architekten, Berlin. Distinctive facade design makes Houses I and III unique: natural wood and the maritime charm of corten steel create a pleasant contrast. To enhance the allusions to shipbuilding and watercraft, Houses I and III possess an extraordinary trapezoidal shape. The arrangement of the living areas at the corners of each building makes for varied views from every apartment: overlooking the water and bathed in sunlight, you're bound to discover delight in the sparkling play of waves time and again.

House II

House II, from the renowned architectural offices of LOVE (Graz, Austria), impresses with its floating footbridges resembling floating jetties and a facade that opens to the east and west. The western side of House II offers a special facade with modern glass folding walls that can be fully opened up to catch the evening sun. Residents of House II enjoy a light and open atmosphere with waterside views direct over the river from large balconies and terraces connected by 'floating jetties' suspended above the ground. This unique footbridge system communicates between the apartments and spacious terraces, providing a common area for recreation and movement.

Location

At 52º Nord, the best of country life brings waterside leisure and recreation together with optimal connections to the city. This is fundamental to the increasing popularity of Treptow-Köpenick in recent years, helped along by positive economic developments in the Adlershof area such as the establishment of nearby science and business development centre WISTA. All the advantages of this top location at a glance:

Perfect connections to city highways (A 113, A 117, A10)

  • Approx. 10 minutes by car to WISTA, Europe's most modern technology park
  • Approx. 15 minutes by car to Berlin's Schönefeld airport
  • Approx. 40 minutes from S-Bahn train station Grünau to Alexanderplatz, Friedrichstrasse, and Berlin central station
